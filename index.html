About Me

My name is Jia Choon (given name) Lee (surname), I am currently a postdoc at BICMR of Peking University, mentored by Qizheng Yin. . I completed my PhD in mathematics at the University of Pennsylvania in 2021 under the supervision of Ron Donagi and Tony Pantev. Prior to that, I grew up in Malaysia and obtained my BSc in mathematics from the University of Edinburgh in 2016.

Research Interests

Algebraic geometry: moduli spaces and their cohomology, Higgs bundles, modules over even Clifford algebras, integrable systems
